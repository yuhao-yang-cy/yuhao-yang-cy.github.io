我是**烤羚羊**（Colin Young），一只吃着老本干国际教育的物理狗，兴趣包含但不限于[物理]({{ site.baseurl }}/blog/category/physics/) 、[数学]({{ site.baseurl }}/blog/category/mathematics/)、[工程技术]({{ site.baseurl }}/blog/category/engineering/)、[人工智能]({{ site.baseurl }}/blog/category/machine-learning/)等等。
	
我在社交平台上的一大爱好就是分享自己原装生产的各种[教案垃圾]({{ site.baseurl }}/projects/)。承蒙师生、家长和网友们的垂青，在网络上积累了些人气，自编的物理讲义（[AS物理]({{ site.baseurl }}/projects/asphysics_notes/) 及[A2物理]({{ site.baseurl }}/projects/a2physics_notes/)）、手写刷题集、[书单推荐]({{ site.baseurl }}/books/)等等，累计下载量已突破大几千，点赞收藏也达六位数之多。我很高兴这些资源能够帮助到相应的受众群体，为了更好地分享和管理它们，我萌生了搭建这个个人网站的想法。
	
在这里，我会把我认为对高中生群体（主要是 A-Level / IB 等国际高中课程的同学）以及其他年龄段的硬科普爱好者们有用的教案、学习资源分享出来。

## Short Bio

Hi! I'm **Colin Young**, a physics enthusiast working in an international education centre based in Shanghai. I have a general interest in STEM subjects, including [physics]({{ site.baseurl }}/blog/category/physics/), [mathematics]({{ site.baseurl }}/blog/category/mathematics/), [engineering science]({{ site.baseurl }}/blog/category/engineering/), [artificial intelligence]({{ site.baseurl }}/blog/category/machine-learning/).

One of my hobbies on the internet is to share various [teaching materials]({{ site.baseurl }}/projects/) that I have created. Thanks to the support of teachers, students, parents, and followers on social media, I've gained some popularity on several platforms. My [lecture notes on AS Physics]({{ site.baseurl }}/projects/asphysics_notes/) and [A-Level Physics]({{ site.baseurl }}/projects/a2physics_notes/), topical revision sheets, written solutions to past papers and [book recommendations]({{ site.baseurl }}/books/) have accumulated tons of likes and downloads. I am happy that these resources are appreciated, and this is the exact reason why I am building this site to make these materials available to whoever finds them useful.

Apart from teaching, I am also a firm practitioner of lifelong learning. I have been a keen reader of popular science and other non-fiction literature. I have taught myself LaTeX (for creating lecture slides and hand-outs) and Python programming skills (for implementing scientific simulations and reproducing simple classic games). I am a badminton player and half-marathon runner.
