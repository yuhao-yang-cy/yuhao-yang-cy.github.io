## This Site Is Under Construction

Hi! I'm Colin Young, a physics enthusiast working in an international education centre based in Shanghai.

One of my main hobbies on the internet is to share various teaching materials that I have created. Thanks to the support of teachers, students, parents, and followers on social media, I've gained some popularity on several platforms. My lecture notes on A-Level physics, topical revision sheets, written solutions to past papers and book recommendations have accumulated hundreds of thousands of likes and downloads. I am happy that these resources are appreciated, and this is the exact reason why I built this site to make these materials available to whoever finds them useful.

For those who are interested, here is more about my personal stories. I have been also enjoying doing mathematical and physical sciences. Nevertheless, after completing a bachelor’s degree in physics at Fudan University followed by a master’s degree in theoretical physics at Imperial College, I became crystal clear that my talents in mathematics and physics are far from what are needed to become a research scienctist, so I decided to pass on my love for physics to the younger generation. And so, my teaching career in international secondary education began in 2011.

Since then, I have been teaching several A-Level syllabuses, including physics, pure mathematics, mechanics, and decision theory, during which I have gained experience and understanding in curriculum design, classroom management and student assessment. Personally, I enjoy transforming my teaching plans into beautifully organized notes or lecture slides, sometimes just to give myself a goal, and this comes with a delightful bonus that these teaching materials that I have created have helped many students to succeed in their courses.

I have also helped students with their university applications, among which there are offer holders of Cambridge, Oxford, Imperial, UCL, KCL, Warwick, Manchester, Bristol, Edinburgh, etc. I helped transform students’ personal statements, from which I enjoyed learning about new things and brainstorming ideas. I also developed a series of systematic lectures for Oxford applicants to succeed in ESAT and TMUA tests (previously PAT and MAT tests), together with a set of Oxbridge mock interview questions for different subject options. These have now become part of my job that I find extremely exciting and refreshing.

I am also a firm practitioner of lifelong learning. I have been a keen reader of popular science and other non-fiction literature. I have taught myself LaTeX (for creating lecture slides and hand-outs) and Python programming skills (for implementing scientific simulations and reproducing simple classic games). I am a badminton player and half-marathon runner.

我是烤羚羊（Colin Young），一只成天吃着老本干国际教育的物理狗。
	
我在社交平台上的一大爱好就是分享自己原装生产的各种教案垃圾。承蒙师生家长和网友们的垂青，几个平台都积累了些人气，自编的物理讲义、手写刷题集、书单推荐等等，累计下载量已突破大几千，点赞收藏也是早已是六位数。我很高兴这些资源受人喜爱，为了更好地分享和管理它们，我萌生了搭建个人网站的想法，而你目前看到的就是尚在施工中的作业现场。
	
在这个个人站上，我会把我认为对高中生群体（主要是 A-Level / IB 等国际高中课程的同学）以及其他年龄段的硬科普爱好者们有用的教案、学习资源分享出来。
