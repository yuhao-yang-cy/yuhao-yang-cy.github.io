Colin Young's Personal Site

我是烤羚羊（Colin Young），一只吃着老本干国际教育的物理狗，兴趣包含但不限于物理 、数学、工程技术、人工智能等等。

我在社交平台上的一大爱好就是分享自己原装生产的各种教案垃圾。承蒙师生、家长和网友们的垂青，在网络上积累了些人气，自编的物理讲义（AS物理 及A2物理）、手写刷题集、书单推荐等等，累计下载量已突破大几千，点赞收藏也达六位数之多。我很高兴这些资源能够帮助到相应的受众群体，为了更好地分享和管理它们，我萌生了搭建这个个人网站的想法。

在这里，我会把我认为对高中生群体（主要是 A-Level / IB 等国际高中课程的同学）以及其他年龄段的硬科普爱好者们有用的教案、学习资源分享出来。

Short Bio

Hi! I’m Colin Young, a physics enthusiast working in an international education centre based in Shanghai. I have a general interest in STEM subjects, including physics, mathematics, engineering science, artificial intelligence.

One of my hobbies on the internet is to share various teaching materials that I have created. Thanks to the support of teachers, students, parents, and followers on social media, I’ve gained some popularity on several platforms. My lecture notes on AS Physics and A-Level Physics, topical revision sheets, written solutions to past papers and book recommendations have accumulated tons of likes and downloads. I am happy that these resources are appreciated, and this is the exact reason why I am building this site to make these materials available to whoever finds them useful.

Apart from teaching, I am also a firm practitioner of lifelong learning. I have been a keen reader of popular science and other non-fiction literature. I have taught myself LaTeX (for creating lecture slides and hand-outs) and Python programming skills (for implementing scientific simulations and reproducing simple classic games). I am a badminton player and half-marathon runner.
